# Hide My Tab from Teachers

Ghost provides privacy-oriented launch and cloaking tools.

## About:Blank Launch

- Open Ghost in an `about:blank` context to reduce visibility in common school monitoring workflows.

## Tab Cloaking

- In Settings, choose a tab cloak (title and icon).
- This can make the tab appear as a standard educational site.

## Important Note

- Behavior depends on school infrastructure and policy enforcement methods. No method guarantees bypass in all environments.
