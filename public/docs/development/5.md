# How to Host Your Own Wisp Endpoint

Hosting your own Wisp endpoint gives you control over your tunnel backend.

## Standard Workflow

1. Open the Wisp server repository: [ghostproxyofficial/wisp-server](https://github.com/ghostproxyofficial/wisp-server).
2. Follow the `README.md` setup instructions in that repository.
3. Deploy your Wisp server to your preferred host.
4. Copy your endpoint URL.
5. In Ghost settings, set your Wisp endpoint to your deployed URL.

## Recommendation

- Keep your endpoint private and stable for best reliability.
